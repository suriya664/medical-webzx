About Dr. John Smith

Dr. John Smith is a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in cardiovascular medicine. He specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases, cardiac surgery, and preventive cardiology.

Dr. Smith completed his medical degree from Harvard Medical School and his residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital. He has published numerous research papers in leading cardiology journals and is a member of the American College of Cardiology.

Education & Qualifications

  • MD - Harvard Medical School
  • Residency - Johns Hopkins Hospital
  • Fellowship - Cardiology, Mayo Clinic
  • Board Certified - American Board of Cardiology

Specializations

  • Cardiac Surgery
  • Preventive Cardiology
  • Heart Failure Management
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
