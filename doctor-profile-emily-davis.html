About Dr. Emily Davis

Dr. Emily Davis is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon with over 14 years of experience in musculoskeletal medicine. She specializes in joint replacement surgery, sports medicine, and minimally invasive orthopedic procedures.

Dr. Davis completed her medical degree from Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons and her orthopedic surgery residency at Hospital for Special Surgery. She is a member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons and has expertise in arthroscopic surgery and joint preservation techniques.

Education & Qualifications

  • MD - Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons
  • Residency - Hospital for Special Surgery
  • Fellowship - Sports Medicine, Steadman Clinic
  • Board Certified - American Board of Orthopaedic Surgery

Specializations

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Sports Medicine
  • Arthroscopic Surgery
  • Fracture Management
