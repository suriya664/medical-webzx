About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified neurologist with over 12 years of experience in treating neurological disorders. She specializes in stroke management, epilepsy, movement disorders, and neurodegenerative diseases.

Dr. Johnson completed her medical degree from Stanford University School of Medicine and her neurology residency at Massachusetts General Hospital. She is a member of the American Academy of Neurology and has contributed to numerous research publications on stroke prevention and treatment.

Education & Qualifications

  • MD - Stanford University School of Medicine
  • Residency - Massachusetts General Hospital
  • Fellowship - Neurocritical Care, Johns Hopkins
  • Board Certified - American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology

Specializations

  • Stroke Management
  • Epilepsy Treatment
  • Movement Disorders
  • Neurodegenerative Diseases
